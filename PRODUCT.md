# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual solo players (including the developer) looking for a quick, low-stakes puzzle break. No accounts, no stakes, session length is a few minutes at most.

## Product Purpose

A single-page "Lights Out" puzzle: toggle lights on an NxN grid until all are off. It's a personal hobby project being revived and modernized. Success looks like a satisfying, responsive puzzle-solving loop with clear feedback on every click, and a puzzle that is always genuinely solvable.

## Brand Personality

Playful, minimal, arcade-tactile. Not soft or corporate — a compact puzzle toy: clean dark UI, one bright accent color for the "on" state, satisfying click feedback.

## Anti-references

Not a marketing or landing page — no hero copy, badges, or dashboard chrome. Avoid the generic "Bootstrap default" look: flat ungraded buttons, low contrast, no visual hierarchy.

## Design Principles

- Clarity over decoration — grid state must be readable at a glance
- Feedback on every interaction — clicks feel tactile and instant
- Fair by construction — every generated puzzle is solvable, no dead ends
- Scales gracefully — same feel from a 3x3 board to an 8x8 board
- Small and fast — no dependency weight beyond what the game needs

## Accessibility & Inclusion

General good-practice bar, no formal WCAG target: sufficient color contrast between on/off states, visible focus states for keyboard users, no motion-only signaling of state.
